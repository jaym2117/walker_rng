Buyout Formula


There are 4 steps to calculating the Buyout Price.


  • 01. 5 Year Average Cash Flows

    Calculate the Average of Cash Flows of the preceeding 5 years from the point which you plan to initiate a buyout.

  • 02. Net Present Value

    Using the average, calculate the net present value of the average cash flows for the remainder of the project.

  • 03. 5 X Average Cash Flows Vs. NPV

    The buyout price will be the greater of 5 X Average Cash Flows and the Net Present Value calculate in the previous step.

  • 04. Discount Price

    Based on whether DLS or Walker initiated the buyout. Discount the NPV calculated.
